Privacy Policy
----------
This application does not collect any personal data:

- No user information is stored or transmitted
- No cookies or tracking technologies are used
- All functionality operates entirely on your local device
- No data is shared with third parties
- The numbers you enter are not saved, logged, or transmitted anywhere

This is a simple, standalone desktop application that requires no data collection to function.
